## Section 2: The Business Models (Pages 6-35)

### Category A: AI Agencies (Low Barrier)
1. **AI Customer Support Agency**
   - What: Set up intelligent chatbots for local businesses
   - Tools: Chatbase, Botpress, Voiceflow, or GPT-4o mini for cost efficiency
   - Pricing: $500-2,000 setup + $200-500/mo
   - Time to first $: 2-4 weeks
   - 2026 angle: Voice-enabled agents are now affordable — upsell phone answering

2. **AI Content Agency**
   - What: AI-assisted blog/social/video content
   - Tools: Claude 3.5, Gemini 2.0, Perplexity for research
   - Pricing: $1,000-3,000/mo retainers
   - Time to first $: 1-2 weeks
   - 2026 angle: Short-form video scripts + AI avatars are in high demand

3. **AI Automation Agency**
   - What: Connect apps, automate workflows with AI decision-making
   - Tools: Make, n8n (now with AI modules), Relevance AI
   - Pricing: $1,500-5,000 per project
   - Time to first $: 2-3 weeks
   - 2026 angle: AI agents that don't just automate — they make decisions

4. **AI Review Management**
   - What: Monitor and respond to reviews
   - Tools: Custom + OpenAI API
   - Pricing: $300-800/mo
   - Time to first $: 1 week

5. **AI Lead Qualification**
   - What: Qualify leads via WhatsApp/SMS
   - Tools: Custom + WhatsApp API
   - Pricing: $500-1,500/mo
   - Time to first $: 2-3 weeks

### Category B: AI Products (Medium Barrier)
6. **AI Email Assistant**
   - What: Write, optimize, and personalize emails at scale
   - Tools: Next.js + GPT-4o or Claude 3.5
   - Pricing: $19-49/mo
   - Time to first $: 4-6 weeks
   - 2026 angle: Hyper-personalization using LinkedIn + company data scraping

7. **AI Meeting Summarizer**
   - What: Summarize Zoom/Teams/Meet calls with action items
   - Tools: Whisper API + Claude 3.5 or Gemini 2.0
   - Pricing: $15-39/mo
   - Time to first $: 3-5 weeks
   - 2026 angle: Real-time coaching suggestions during calls

8. **AI Proposal Generator**
   - What: Generate client proposals
   - Tools: Next.js + OpenAI
   - Pricing: $29-79/mo
   - Time to first $: 3-4 weeks

9. **AI Social Media Manager**
   - What: Schedule + generate posts
   - Tools: Next.js + multiple APIs
   - Pricing: $39-99/mo
   - Time to first $: 4-6 weeks

10. **AI Resume Optimizer**
    - What: Tailor resumes + auto-apply to matching jobs
    - Tools: Next.js + Claude 3.5
    - Pricing: $19-39/mo
    - Time to first $: 2-3 weeks
    - 2026 angle: ATS optimization is now table stakes — add LinkedIn profile optimization
