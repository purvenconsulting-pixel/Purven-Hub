# AI Side Hustle Blueprint — Launch Plan

## Product Positioning

**Target Customer:** Working professionals aged 25-45 who want extra income but don't know how to monetize AI.

**Problem:** They see AI everywhere, feel left behind, and don't know where to start or how to make money with it.

**Solution:** A step-by-step blueprint showing exactly how to earn their first $1,000 using free AI tools.

**Differentiator:** Not theory — a proven 30-day action plan with templates, prompts, and real case studies.

**Urgency:** The AI opportunity window is open now. Early movers become the experts.

**Positioning Statement:**
"For working professionals who want extra income, The AI Side Hustle Blueprint is a step-by-step guide that shows you how to earn your first $1,000 using free AI tools. Unlike generic 'make money online' courses, we give you the exact 30-day roadmap, 100+ proven prompts, and real case studies from people who've done it."

### Stage 1: Awareness
**Channels:**
- Twitter/X threads about AI income
- TikTok/Shorts: "How I made $X with AI"
- Reddit: r/sidehustle value posts
- SEO blog: "AI side hustle" keywords

**Content ideas:**
- "7 AI tools that pay for themselves"
- "I made $1,000 in 30 days with ChatGPT"
- "The AI side hustle nobody is talking about"
- "Free AI tools that replace expensive software"

### Stage 2: Interest
**Lead Magnet:** "7 AI Tools That Pay for Themselves" (free PDF, 10 pages)
**Landing page:** Simple Carrd page with email opt-in
**Delivery:** Instant PDF + welcome email sequence

### Stage 3: Consideration
**Email sequence (5 emails):**
1. Deliver lead magnet + introduce yourself
2. Share your AI income story
3. The 7 models deep dive
4. Case studies and testimonials
5. Soft pitch the blueprint

### Stage 4: Conversion
**Sales page elements:**
- Headline: "The Step-by-Step Blueprint to Your First $1,000 with AI"
- Subheadline: "No coding. No investment. Just free tools and a proven 30-day plan."
- Problem agitation
- Solution preview
- What's included (visual)
- Testimonials
- Pricing tiers
- FAQ
- Guarantee (30-day money back)
- Final CTA

### Stage 5: Retention
**Post-purchase:**
- Welcome email with download links
- 30-day action plan emails
- Community invite (VIP)
- Upsell to community/coaching
