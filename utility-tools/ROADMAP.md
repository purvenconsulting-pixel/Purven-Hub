### Phase 1 Traffic (Months 1-2)

- **Social**: Share tool results on LinkedIn, Twitter
- **Forums**: Answer questions on Reddit r/marketing, r/SEO

### Phase 2 Traffic (Months 2-3)
- **SEO**: Professional keywords ("LinkedIn headline examples")
